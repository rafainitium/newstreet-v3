Newstreet is developing a 48-unit mixed-use multifamily building at Clark and Wrightwood in East Lincoln Park.

The project was entitled to include 38 market-rate units, 10 affordable units at 60% AMI, ground-floor retail, and integrated amenities designed for modern urban living.
